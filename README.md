🍽️ Canteen Mate 📌 Overview

Canteen Mate is a smart pre-ordering system designed for college and office canteens. It allows users to place food orders before the lunch break, helping reduce long queues and overcrowding in the canteen.

The app ensures a smoother, faster, and more organized food service experience for both customers and canteen staff.

During lunch breaks, canteens face:

Canteen Mate solves this by enabling pre-ordering, making the process fast and organized.

💭 What is Canteen Mate?

Ever stood in a long canteen line while your break time just disappears? Yeah… we’ve all been there 😩

Canteen Mate is our solution to that problem. It lets you order food before the lunch break, so instead of waiting in a crowd, you just walk in, pick up your food, and chill.

🚀 Why we built this

Canteens get super crowded during lunch breaks:

Long queues Wasted time Slow service

We thought… why not make it smarter?

So we built Canteen Mate — a simple system where ordering happens before the rush.

✨ What you can do 📝 Pre-order your food in advance 📋 Browse the canteen menu anytime ⏰ Select your pickup time 🔔 Get notified when your order is ready 🚶 Walk in, pick up, and leave — no waiting ⚙️ How it works Open the app Choose your food items Select a pickup time (before lunch break) Place your order Come to the canteen and collect it
